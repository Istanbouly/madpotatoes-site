We’re Mad Potatoes

Born from a love of baked potatoes and big personalities, Mad Potatoes hit the streets with one mission: turn the humble spud into something unforgettable. We serve up hot, fluffy potatoes loaded with bold, customizable toppings — all from our lively little food truck.

Toppings With Attitude

Whether you're into gooey cheese, spicy jalapeños, crispy chicken, or plant-based goodness — we've got the fixings to match your flavor mood. Think of our menu as a playground for your taste buds, built for fun, fast bites with real flavor.

Powered by Passion

Behind the scenes, we’re a crew of spud lovers who care about good food and good vibes. Every potato is prepped with care, served with a smile, and meant to bring people together — one delicious bite at a time.

Our Values

We believe in quality, customer satisfaction, and creativity. Every potato we serve is made with fresh ingredients, and our staff is always ready to deliver a smile with every order. We are committed to sustainability and try to source our ingredients responsibly. We also believe in creating a community, not just serving food. Our customers are the heart of our business, and we constantly strive to create an experience that leaves them coming back for more.
